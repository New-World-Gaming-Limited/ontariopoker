GGPoker Freerolls

GGPoker offers the most varied free tournament schedule of any Ontario room. The headline event is the Sunday C$2,500 freeroll, which draws a large field but provides meaningful prize money at the top of the payout structure. For players with the time to grind, this is the best single weekly freeroll available on any Ontario platform.

The daily schedule includes a C$500 freeroll at 12pm ET every day, open to all registered players. The field is large, but top finishers collect real cash with no buy-in required.

Beyond the standard schedule, GGPoker also runs Ontario MILLION$ feeder freerolls that award seats to higher buy-in qualifiers. Players can build a path from zero to a tournament seat without investing any money if they run deep in these events. During active series periods, BHS satellite freerolls also appear in the lobby, giving players an entry point into the Bounty Hunters Series qualifier ladder.

To find GGPoker freerolls: open the tournament tab, filter by "Freeroll" in the tournament type dropdown. They are always listed with a C$0 entry fee.

888poker Freerolls

888poker runs a daily $1,000 freeroll that is open to all registered players. It runs on a consistent daily schedule, making it easy to build it into a regular routine. The prize pool is split across a standard payout structure, with the top finishers taking the largest share.

New players get access to dedicated new player freerolls in the weeks following registration. These have smaller fields than the general daily freeroll, which improves your chances of cashing. 888poker also runs Made to Play freerolls tied to their recreational player promotion, which are available to players in the Made to Play program.

To find 888poker freerolls: click on "Tournaments" in the lobby and select "Free" from the buy-in filter.

PokerStars Freerolls

PokerStars runs multiple daily freerolls across different times of day. These are listed in the tournament lobby under the Freeroll filter. The prizes range from small cash amounts to tournament tickets for other events on the platform.

PokerStars also offers Spin & Go freerolls, which are free entries to the Spin & Go jackpot format. These are awarded through promotions and challenges rather than sitting permanently in the lobby. During ONCOOP (Ontario Championship of Online Poker) season, ONCOOP qualifier freerolls are added to the schedule, providing a zero-cost path to ONCOOP events.

PokerStars has the deepest overall tournament volume of any Ontario room, which means their freeroll schedule can be busier than other platforms during series periods. Check the lobby directly for the current week's freeroll listing.

BetMGM Poker Freerolls

BetMGM Poker runs a weekly $500 freeroll as the anchor of their free tournament schedule. This runs on a fixed day each week and is open to all registered players. BetMGM also offers Grind Rewards freerolls, which are available to players who have accumulated points in the Grind Rewards BRP (Bonus Reward Points) ladder.

The Grind Rewards freerolls have smaller fields because eligibility is tied to recent play activity. For players who are already active on BetMGM, these offer better expected value than the open weekly freeroll. Check your Grind Rewards status in the account section to see which freerolls you qualify for.

PartyPoker Freerolls

PartyPoker offers a daily $250 freeroll alongside regular SPINS freerolls that award tickets to their jackpot Sit & Go format. The daily freeroll runs on a consistent schedule, and the prize pool, while modest, is real cash awarded to top finishers.

PartyPoker's SPINS freerolls are tied to specific promotions and may not run every week. Check the tournament lobby for the current schedule. PartyPoker and BetMGM share the same player pool on the ElectraWorks Maple network, but freerolls are listed separately per platform.

Bwin Freerolls

Bwin operates on the same ElectraWorks Maple network as PartyPoker and BetMGM. Their freeroll offering follows a similar schedule to PartyPoker, with daily and weekly free events visible in the tournament lobby. Because all three platforms share the same underlying network, freeroll pools and timing often mirror one another.

Players who have accounts on multiple ElectraWorks Maple platforms should note that freerolls are credited per platform, not shared across accounts.

Tips for Getting the Most from Freerolls

Freerolls attract large fields, which changes optimal strategy compared to paid tournaments. Early stages see loose play from players with no financial risk. Tight, patient play in the first few levels tends to produce better results than aggressive early gambles.

The most reliable bankroll-building path through freerolls is winning tournament tickets rather than cash. Tickets to $5-$10 tournaments are often easier to win than cash and act as a stepping stone to the paid schedule. GGPoker's feeder freerolls for the Ontario MILLION$ are the best example of this pathway on any Ontario platform.

If you have limited time, prioritise the Sunday C$2,500 freeroll on GGPoker and the daily $1K on 888poker. These offer the best prize-to-field ratios of the recurring freerolls across all six Ontario rooms.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a freeroll in online poker?
A freeroll is a tournament that costs nothing to enter but awards real cash prizes or tickets to other tournaments. Ontario's regulated rooms run freerolls as part of their standard schedule, ranging from daily small-prize events to larger weekly tournaments with C$1,000+ prize pools.
How do I find freerolls on Ontario poker sites?
Each room lists freerolls in their tournament lobby. On GGPoker, filter by "Freeroll" in the tournament tab. On 888poker, select the "Free" category. On PokerStars, use the freeroll filter in the tournament finder. Most daily freerolls run on a fixed schedule, so checking the lobby once is enough to know the recurring times.
Who is eligible for Ontario poker freerolls?
Standard daily freerolls are open to all registered players physically located in Ontario at the time of play. Some freerolls, like new player events or loyalty freerolls, require meeting specific criteria such as recent deposit activity or VIP status. Check each room's lobby for entry requirements before registering.
Can freerolls actually help build a bankroll?
Yes, but modestly. Freerolls have large fields and small prizes per player, so consistent cashes are difficult. The real value is in winning tickets to paid tournaments. GGPoker's Sunday C$2,500 freeroll and 888poker's daily $1K freeroll are the best options for meaningful prize money with no buy-in required.
