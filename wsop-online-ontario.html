WSOP Comes to Ontario

The World Series of Poker is the most prestigious brand in poker, and Ontario players can now compete for real WSOP Gold Bracelets without leaving the province. GGPoker Ontario (GGPoker.ca) is the exclusive home of WSOP-branded online events in Ontario's regulated market, offering both Bracelet Series and Circuit Series events throughout the year.

This is a significant competitive advantage for GGPoker over the other five regulated Ontario rooms. No other operator in Ontario can offer WSOP-branded events or bracelets.

What Events Are Available?

WSOP Online Gold Bracelet Series

The flagship offering. Run annually (typically aligned with the summer WSOP live schedule in Las Vegas), these are official WSOP events where winners receive genuine Gold Bracelets and their results count toward WSOP Player of the Year standings. Ontario's ring-fenced player pool means smaller fields than global events, which translates to better value for qualified players.

WSOP Online Circuit Series

More frequent than the Bracelet Series, Circuit events run multiple times per year on GGPoker Ontario. Winners receive WSOP Circuit rings and qualify for additional championship events. Buy-ins are generally lower than Bracelet events, making them more accessible for recreational players.

Satellite Qualifiers

GGPoker Ontario runs an extensive satellite structure leading into both Bracelet and Circuit events. Multi-step satellite paths let players start with buy-ins under $10 and work their way up to major event seats. This is one of the most cost-effective ways to play for a WSOP title.

Ontario's Ring-Fenced Advantage

Ontario's regulated market operates a ring-fenced player pool, separate from the rest of the world. For WSOP events, this means:

  • Smaller fields: Hundreds of entries rather than thousands. Your probability of making a final table is significantly higher.
  • Softer competition: The player pool includes a higher proportion of recreational players compared to global GGPoker events.
  • Same prestige: A bracelet won in Ontario carries the same weight as one won in a global event or at the Rio in Las Vegas.

This combination of smaller fields, softer average skill level, and identical prizes makes Ontario one of the best places in the world to pursue a WSOP bracelet online.

How to Qualify for WSOP Events on GGPoker Ontario

  1. Create a GGPoker.ca account. You must be 19+ and physically located in Ontario.
  2. Check the tournament lobby. WSOP events and satellites appear under the "WSOP" tab in the GGPoker Ontario tournament lobby.
  3. Start with satellites. Don't buy in directly to a $200+ event if you can qualify through a $5 satellite.
  4. Build a satellite bankroll. Budget for multiple satellite attempts. Even strong players need several shots to win a seat.
  5. Register early for Bracelet events. Some events have guaranteed prize pools that create overlay when registrations fall short, meaning positive expected value for entrants.
