Why Only Regulated Rooms?

iGaming Ontario tells players to "Make the safer choice and play on sites offered by fully registered and approved Operators in Ontario." That is our editorial policy too. Every poker room on this site is verified against the official iGO operator directory.

The regulated market represents the majority of Ontario's online gambling activity, with a channelization rate of 83.7% in FY2024-25. That means most Ontario players are already choosing regulated sites. This portal covers all six of them, and nothing else.

Ontario's regulated poker vertical generated approximately C$70.2 million of NAGGR in 2025, from C$1.678 billion in wagers. While poker is a small share of the total market (~1.74%), these are high-intent players who value verified information over generic listicles.
