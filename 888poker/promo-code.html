What to Expect After Signing Up

To claim the 888poker Ontario welcome bonus, new players must make a first deposit of C$10 or more using promo code Welcome100. The bonus provides a 100% match up to C$1,000, though players should note a discrepancy in 888poker's public marketing: the page title advertises up to $400 while the page body states up to $1,000. This inconsistency was present during our research and players should verify the current terms directly on the 888poker Ontario bonus page before depositing.

In addition to the deposit match, 888poker offers a separate no-deposit promotion that grants tournament tickets over seven days for new Ontario accounts. Each ticket expires 24 hours after it is issued, so players must log in daily to use their tickets before they lapse. This seven-day drip structure is designed to build a daily login habit during the critical first week of account activity.

The 90-day bonus clearing window gives players three months to release the full matched bonus through play. Beyond the welcome offer, 888poker's ongoing promotions include BLAST leaderboards, freeroll tournaments totaling over C$12,000 weekly, and the rotating Gift Drops, Winner Spinner, and Hand of the Day challenges. Players should check the promotions page regularly as offers are updated frequently.

AGCO Compliance Note

All online poker operators in Ontario must be registered with the Alcohol and Gaming Commission of Ontario (AGCO) and operate under a conducting and managing agreement with iGaming Ontario (iGO). This regulatory framework means that 888poker, operated by VHL Ontario Limited, must adhere to strict standards for player protection, game fairness, responsible gambling, and marketing practices.

Under AGCO rules, advertising that communicates gambling inducements, bonuses, and credits is prohibited except on an operator's own gaming site.
