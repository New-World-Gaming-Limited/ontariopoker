How to Use the Session Tracker

  1. Log each session as soon as you finish playing. Enter the date, room, game type, stakes, duration, buy-in, and cash-out. Add notes about key hands or table conditions.
  2. Check your dashboard to see running totals: total profit or loss, hourly rate, best and worst sessions, win percentage, and your current winning or losing streak.
  3. Review your history with filters. Sort by date, room, or profit. Filter to see only cash games at a specific room, or tournaments in a date range.
  4. Study your charts to spot patterns. The bankroll graph shows your trajectory over time. The room breakdown shows where you're most profitable. The game type chart highlights your strengths.
  5. Export your data regularly. The CSV export works with Excel, Google Sheets, and other tools. You can also import a CSV to restore from a backup or transfer between devices.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my session data private?

Yes. All data is stored locally on your device using window._ls. OntarioPoker never sees, collects, or stores your session data. Nothing is sent to any server.

Can I export my poker session history?

Yes. Click the Export CSV button in the Data tab to download a spreadsheet of all your logged sessions. Open it in Excel, Google Sheets, or any spreadsheet app.

What happens if I clear my browser data?

Clearing your browser data or window._ls will remove your saved sessions. We recommend exporting to CSV regularly as a backup. You can re-import the CSV at any time.

Why should I track my poker sessions?

Tracking sessions helps you identify which games, stakes, and rooms are most profitable. It also helps you spot leaks, manage your bankroll, and make data-driven decisions about where and when to play.

Does this tracker work on mobile?

Yes. The session tracker is fully responsive and works on phones, tablets, and desktops. Your data syncs within the same browser on the same device.
