Withdrawal Methods Compared

E-Wallets (Fastest)

E-wallets are the fastest way to withdraw from Ontario poker rooms. Services like MuchBetter and ecoPayz are widely accepted. GGPoker and 888poker are the quickest to process e-wallet cashouts, often completing requests within hours. E-wallets also allow you to keep funds separate from your bank account, which some players prefer for bankroll management.

Interac e-Transfer

Interac is the most popular withdrawal method for Canadian players. It sends funds directly to your bank account without needing a third-party wallet. Processing times range from 1-3 business days depending on the room. GGPoker and 888poker are at the faster end of that range. Interac is free at most rooms and has reasonable daily limits.

Bank Transfer

Direct bank transfers are the slowest option, typically taking 3-5 business days across all rooms. They're best suited for larger withdrawals where you want funds sent directly to your bank without any intermediary. Some rooms have higher minimum withdrawal amounts for bank transfers.

Visa and Mastercard

Card withdrawals are available at most Ontario rooms but are often slower than Interac or e-wallets, typically 2-5 business days. Some banks may also apply additional processing time. If speed matters to you, Interac or an e-wallet is a better choice.

Tips for Faster Withdrawals

  • Verify your identity early: Complete KYC (Know Your Customer) verification right after you sign up, before you need to withdraw. Upload your ID and proof of address as soon as the room allows it.
  • Withdraw to the same method you deposited with: Rooms often require your first withdrawal to go back to your deposit method. This avoids processing delays.
  • Submit during business hours: Withdrawal requests submitted on weekday mornings (ET) tend to be processed faster than those submitted Friday evening or on weekends.
  • Use e-wallets for speed: If fast cashouts are your top priority, deposit and withdraw via e-wallet. This gives you the fastest turnaround at every room.
  • Check for pending bonuses: If you have active wagering requirements, your withdrawal may be delayed or the bonus voided. Clear all bonus conditions before withdrawing.

What About First-Time Withdrawals?

Your first withdrawal from any Ontario poker room will take longer than subsequent ones. This is because rooms are required by AGCO regulations to verify your identity before processing your first cashout. The verification process (KYC) involves submitting government-issued ID and proof of address. GGPoker handles first-time verification fastest in our experience, typically within a few hours. Other rooms may take 24-48 hours for initial verification.

After your identity is verified, future withdrawals process at the standard speeds listed in our comparison table above. This is another reason to complete your verification early, even before you need to withdraw.

Withdrawal Limits by Room

Each Ontario poker room sets its own daily and monthly withdrawal limits. These limits vary by payment method and VIP/loyalty status. For most recreational players, the standard limits are more than sufficient. High-volume players should check the cashier section of their chosen room for specific limits. If you need to withdraw a large amount, bank transfer typically has the highest maximum, though it is also the slowest method.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which Ontario poker site has the fastest withdrawals?

GGPoker offers the fastest withdrawals in Ontario, with e-wallet cashouts typically processed within a few hours. Their verification process is quick, and first-time withdrawals are handled faster than most competitors.

How long do Interac withdrawals take from Ontario poker sites?

Interac e-Transfer withdrawals from Ontario poker sites typically take 1-3 business days, depending on the room. GGPoker and 888poker tend to process Interac requests faster than other rooms.

Do Ontario poker sites charge withdrawal fees?

Most regulated Ontario poker sites do not charge fees for standard withdrawals. Some rooms may charge for additional withdrawals beyond a monthly free limit, or for specific methods like bank wire. Check each room's cashier page for current fee schedules.

Why is my Ontario poker withdrawal taking so long?

Common reasons include pending identity verification (KYC), withdrawal to a method different from your deposit method, exceeding daily or weekly withdrawal limits, or processing during weekends. Contact the room's support team if your withdrawal has been pending for more than the stated timeframe.
