How to Read This Comparison

All six rooms are fully regulated by iGaming Ontario (iGO) and registered with the AGCO. Ratings reflect our editorial assessment across software quality, tournament offering, cash games, payments, bonus structure, and customer support.

The feature matrix uses green checkmarks (✓) and red crosses (✗) to show feature availability at a glance. Green-highlighted cells indicate category leaders or rare features.

Promo code status reflects what was publicly verifiable at our last check. "Visit operator site for current offers" means no public code was captured, not that the operator has no new-player offer. Under AGCO rules, specific bonus amounts cannot be advertised outside an operator's own site.
