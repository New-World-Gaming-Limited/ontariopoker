Pickering Casino Confirms Four April Poker Dates for Ontario Players

Five tournament flights spanning C$200 to C$1,150, giving GTA-area players a clear weekly rhythm through the end of the month.

Pickering Casino Resort has posted four live poker tournament dates for the rest of April 2026. The schedule runs from a C$575 NLH on April 16 through a C$1,150 Deep Stack on April 30, giving GTA-area players a clear weekly rhythm at one of Ontario's most active live rooms.

Thursdays anchor the bigger buy-ins: C$575 on April 16, C$750 on April 23, and the month's headline event, the C$1,150 Deep Stack, on April 30. Tuesday, April 21 runs two Turbo flights at C$200 each - noon and evening - giving players two shots at the same format without doubling the commitment. The structure suits players who want to take a shot at a major without blocking out their whole day for a single chance.

Where Pickering Fits in Ontario's Live Landscape

Pickering Casino Resort, operated by Great Canadian Entertainment, sits at 1580 Bayly St in Durham Region, about 40 minutes east of downtown Toronto via Highway 401. For most GTA players, it's the closest venue offering a structured weekly tournament calendar, which is a genuine gap in the local market.

The geography matters. Fallsview and Casino Niagara are roughly two hours from Toronto. Playground in Kahnawake, Quebec, is closer to five. Pickering doesn't require an overnight stay or a day-trip commitment, which makes it the default option for players who want regular live tournament reps without the travel overhead.

The room runs cash games alongside its tournament schedule, so players can combine both in a single visit. That flexibility, plus the weekly structure, is what keeps Pickering on the radar even during weeks when the buy-ins are modest.

The venue has also hosted serious events. Great Canadian Toronto generated the third-largest WSOP Circuit prize pool in history - C$3.7 million - in April 2025. The live infrastructure is there, and the April schedule reflects a room that treats poker as a consistent product rather than an occasional event.

The Broader Ontario Tournament Picture

April is one of the busier months on the Ontario poker calendar, and Pickering's schedule sits alongside several other active events. The GGPoker Bounty Hunters Series Ontario runs through April 27 online. Today (April 12) is the Final Stage of the Ontario MILLION$ C$100K GTD, and the Mystery Bounty Main Event with a C$250K GTD is set for April 27.

Looking ahead, Casino Niagara hosts its Spring Main Event from May 19 to 22, and WSOP Circuit Playground returns May 10 to 25. For players working through a live calendar, May is the logical next step after the Pickering Deep Stack on April 30. The full Ontario poker tournament schedule covers both months in detail.
