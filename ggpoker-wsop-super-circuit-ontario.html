About Playground Poker Club

Playground Poker Club is located at 1500 Route 138, Kahnawake, QC, roughly 500km from Toronto, about a five-hour drive. It operates under the Kahnawake Gaming Commission and is the largest poker room in Canada by capacity.

Playground has hosted WSOP Circuit events since 2022 and set the record for the largest WSOP Circuit Main Event prize pool ever, C$4,450,500 in August 2025, drawn by 1,978 entries. The April 2026 series produced the second-largest, at C$4,042,870 with 1,781 entries. Both records were set at Playground.

The venue's trajectory has been clear. Andrew Johnson, Playground's Assistant Director of Poker Operations, said of the Super Circuit announcement: "It's going to be the biggest tournament Canada has ever seen, and that's a fact."

For Ontario players, Playground is the default destination for major live tournaments. WSOP Circuit events at Playground attract fields from across Canada and the United States, and the qualifier infrastructure through GGPoker means Ontario players have the same access as anyone else.

What Is the WSOP Super Circuit?

The World Series of Poker Super Circuit is a higher-tier version of the standard WSOP Circuit. Standard Circuit stops typically feature a Main Event with a guarantee in the C$500K to C$2M range. Super Circuit stops raise that significantly, with the Canada edition guaranteeing C$10M, the equivalent of a major international stop.

Super Circuit events award WSOP gold rings (the same prestige level as the regular Circuit), and the winner of the Main Event earns automatic qualification to the WSOP Player of the Year standings. It's the same WSOP branding, structure, and sanctioning as a Las Vegas event, held in Canada for the first time.
