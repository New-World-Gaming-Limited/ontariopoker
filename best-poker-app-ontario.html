Last updated March 2026

Every regulated Ontario poker room offers a mobile app or browser-based mobile experience. We tested all six on both iOS and Android, measuring load times, feature parity, stability, and overall usability. GGPoker's app came out on top, but several rooms deliver strong mobile experiences worth considering.

1. GGPoker: Best Overall Poker App in Ontario

Our Pick: Best Mobile Poker App

GGPoker's mobile app is purpose-built rather than a downsized desktop client. It launches faster than any other Ontario poker app we tested, and the interface adapts cleanly to both phone and tablet screens. The Smart HUD, which shows real-time statistics on opponents, works natively on mobile, a feature no other Ontario room matches. PokerCraft analytics are accessible in-app, letting you review hand histories and session data without switching to a browser.

Other standout mobile features include All-in Insurance, Spin & Gold jackpot sit-and-gos, and one-tap tournament registration. Push notifications keep you informed about late registration windows and upcoming events. The app supports both portrait and landscape play.

Read the full GGPoker review | Visit GGPoker

2. 888poker: Best for Social Mobile Play

888poker's app is clean and well-organized, with dedicated sections for cash games, tournaments, and BLAST fast-fold poker. The standout mobile feature is webcam tables, which let you see opponents via your phone's camera. Snap Poker (their fast-fold format) works smoothly on mobile with swipe-to-fold gestures. The app loads quickly, though not quite as fast as GGPoker's. Tournament filtering and registration are intuitive on smaller screens.

Read the full 888poker review | Visit 888poker

3. BetMGM Poker: Best for Cross-Platform Players

BetMGM's poker app is part of its larger sports and casino ecosystem. If you already use BetMGM for sports betting, adding poker is seamless, with a single wallet and unified account. The poker interface itself is solid if not spectacular, with functional table layouts and smooth multi-table support (up to four tables on tablets). Tournament lobbies load quickly and the registration process is straightforward. MGM Rewards points earned through poker can be used across all BetMGM products.

Read the full BetMGM review | Visit BetMGM Poker

4. PokerStars: Best for Game Variety on Mobile

PokerStars delivers the widest range of game types on mobile, including Zoom Poker, Spin & Go, and various mixed-game formats. The app is mature and polished, benefiting from years of global development. Multi-tabling is well supported, with up to four tables on phone and six on tablet. The lobby filters are detailed, making it easy to find specific game types and stakes. One trade-off: the app is larger than most competitors, so it takes more storage space and slightly longer to download.

Read the full PokerStars review | Visit PokerStars

5. PartyPoker: Functional Mobile Client

PartyPoker's app shares the same network as BetMGM and Bwin, so you're accessing the same player pool. The interface is functional and covers the basics well: cash games, tournaments, and their fastforward quick-fold format. Table layouts are clear, and the app handles portrait mode competently. The design is a step behind GGPoker and PokerStars in terms of polish, but it gets the job done without crashes or notable lag.

Read the full PartyPoker review | Visit PartyPoker

6. Bwin: Mobile Browser Only

Bwin doesn't offer a dedicated poker app on iOS or Android. Instead, poker is accessible through the mobile browser. This means no App Store or Play Store download is required, which some players prefer. The browser experience is adequate for casual play, though it lacks the speed and polish of native apps. It runs on the same shared network as PartyPoker and BetMGM, so the game selection and player pool are identical. If you mainly play on desktop and only occasionally play on your phone, Bwin's browser approach may be sufficient.

Read the full Bwin review | Visit Bwin

Key Features to Look for in an Ontario Poker App

When choosing a mobile poker room, consider these factors:

  • Multi-table support: How many tables can you play at once? GGPoker and PokerStars lead here with smooth multi-tabling on both phone and tablet.
  • Fast-fold poker: For quick sessions on the go, fast-fold variants are essential. All rooms except Bwin offer a mobile-optimized fast-fold format.
  • Push notifications: Tournament reminders, late reg alerts, and promotion updates help you stay on top of your schedule.
  • Portrait vs. landscape: Some players prefer single-hand portrait mode on their commute. GGPoker and 888poker handle portrait mode particularly well.
  • Geolocation: All apps require location services to confirm you're in Ontario. Make sure your device's GPS is enabled before opening the app.

How We Tested

We installed every app on both an iPhone 15 and a Samsung Galaxy S24 running the latest OS versions. We measured cold-start launch times, tested multi-table performance under load, played at least 200 hands on each app, and evaluated the tournament registration flow on small screens. Testing was completed in March 2026 from Toronto, Ontario.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which Ontario poker app has the best mobile experience?

GGPoker offers the best mobile poker experience in Ontario, with a purpose-built app featuring Smart HUD, PokerCraft analytics, and fast load times on both iOS and Android.

Can I play on multiple Ontario poker apps at once?

Yes. Ontario players can install and play on multiple regulated poker apps. You can have accounts at all six licensed rooms and switch between them freely.

Do Ontario poker apps work on tablets?

All six Ontario poker apps work on tablets. GGPoker, 888poker, BetMGM, and PokerStars have dedicated tablet-optimized layouts. PartyPoker and Bwin scale their mobile interfaces for larger screens.

Is geolocation required for Ontario poker apps?

Yes. All regulated Ontario poker apps use geolocation to verify you are physically in Ontario before allowing real-money play. You must enable location services on your device.
