Overview

PartyPoker Ontario is operated by ElectraWorks Maple Limited as agent of iGaming Ontario. The room holds a valid operator agreement with iGaming Ontario and is subject to oversight by the Alcohol and Gaming Commission of Ontario. To play, you must be 19 or older and physically located in Ontario.

The PartyPoker brand has been running online poker since 2001 and was among the original major rooms in the industry. The Ontario-regulated product launched in April 2022 as part of the wave of operators that entered when iGaming Ontario opened. PartyPoker Ontario is part of the Entain Group family and shares its Ontario player pool with BetMGM Poker Ontario and Bwin Ontario - all three are operated under the same ElectraWorks Maple Limited entity.

The room scores 3.9/5.0 in our full review. Traffic sits below GGPoker and PokerStars, but the network arrangement means the combined PartyPoker/BetMGM/Bwin pool is larger than any of the three brands individually. Key formats include Fast Forward fast-fold cash games, SPINS jackpot sit-and-gos, and a cashback rewards program.

Software and Platform Features

PartyPoker Ontario uses the Entain Group's proprietary poker platform, adapted for the Ontario regulated market. Desktop software is available for Windows and Mac. Mobile play is available through the iOS and Android apps. The interface is clean and functional, with tournament lobbies, hand history, and support tools accessible from the main client.

Fast Forward

Fast Forward is PartyPoker's fast-fold cash game format. When you fold, you are immediately transferred to a fresh table with different opponents. This removes the waiting time that standard ring game players experience between hands. Fast Forward tables run at several stake levels for No Limit Hold'em. The format suits players looking to put in volume in limited time, or players who find waiting on opponents tedious.

SPINS

SPINS are three-player jackpot sit-and-gos. Before each game starts, a random multiplier is applied to the prize pool. Standard multipliers produce modest returns, while rare higher multipliers produce large payouts relative to the buy-in. Games complete quickly - typically under ten minutes. SPINS are available at various buy-in levels and are one of PartyPoker's most-played formats globally.

Daily Legends Tournaments

The Daily Legends schedule runs guaranteed tournaments each day across different buy-in levels. Each Legends event has a defined structure and guarantee published in advance. The schedule covers No Limit Hold'em and includes some mixed-game variants depending on the day. These events form the anchor of PartyPoker Ontario's daily tournament programme.

The Shared Network Explained

This is the most important thing to understand about PartyPoker Ontario: it shares a player pool with BetMGM Poker Ontario and Bwin Ontario. All three rooms are operated by ElectraWorks Maple Limited under iGaming Ontario's framework. When you sit at a cash table or register for a tournament on PartyPoker Ontario, you are competing against players who registered through any of the three brands.

This arrangement has a practical upside. Traffic on any single brand may appear modest, but the combined pool across three brands produces more consistent table fill rates than the individual numbers suggest. Players comparing lobby counts between PartyPoker and GGPoker should be aware that PartyPoker's figures represent only one third of its actual available player pool.

Cash Games

PartyPoker Ontario's cash game tables cover No Limit Hold'em at micro, low, and mid stakes. Pot Limit Omaha is available at select stakes. Standard ring tables and Fast Forward tables both populate the cash lobby. During peak hours, multiple tables run simultaneously at the popular stake levels. Off-peak, Fast Forward is the most reliable format for avoiding long wait times, since it requires fewer active players to run.

The shared player pool with BetMGM Poker and Bwin means that cash game traffic is collectively higher than what any single brand's lobby count would suggest. If you are evaluating tables and notice PartyPoker's numbers appear lower than expected, this is the reason. You are seeing one brand's count, not the network's total.

Tournaments

The tournament schedule at PartyPoker Ontario is narrower than PokerStars Ontario's offering but covers the major formats. Daily Legends events run at set times each day with defined buy-ins and guarantees. The schedule includes early, afternoon, and evening start times. SPINS run continuously on demand. Satellites for larger events appear periodically depending on the series calendar.

For players who primarily want tournaments rather than cash games, the comparison with GGPoker and PokerStars is relevant. See GGPoker vs PartyPoker for a structured breakdown of tournament depth across the two rooms.

Cashback Rewards

PartyPoker Ontario operates a cashback program for regular players. Cashback is calculated on rake paid in cash games and accrued over rolling periods. The current rate and tier structure are set by the operator and visible within the platform's promotions section. Under AGCO regulations, specific promotional amounts cannot be advertised on third-party sites - visit PartyPoker Ontario directly for current cashback details.

Cashback is one of the cleaner ways to receive ongoing value from a poker room, since the return is straightforward rather than subject to playthrough conditions. Players who put in consistent cash game volume will find the cashback structure more meaningful than infrequent tournament players.

Getting Started

Registration is completed on the PartyPoker Ontario platform. You must confirm you are 19 or older and physically located in Ontario at the time of play. Have a government-issued ID ready for the verification step, which is required before withdrawals are processed.

  1. Visit PartyPoker Ontario and click "Register."
  2. Enter your name, email, date of birth, and Ontario address.
  3. Choose a username.
  4. Accept the terms and confirm your Ontario location.
  5. Download the app or desktop client, or continue via the mobile browser.
  6. Complete identity verification when prompted - upload a government-issued ID.
  7. Make a deposit using a supported Ontario payment method.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is PartyPoker Ontario legally regulated?
Yes. It is operated by ElectraWorks Maple Limited under an operator agreement with iGaming Ontario and regulated by the AGCO. You must be 19+ and physically located in Ontario to play.
Does PartyPoker Ontario share tables with BetMGM Poker and Bwin?
Yes. All three rooms share a single player pool for cash games and tournaments. Registering at any one of them gives access to the same tables and opponents.
What is the Fast Forward format?
Fast Forward is PartyPoker's fast-fold cash game. When you fold, you move to a new table immediately with different opponents. It is available at multiple stakes levels and suits players looking to increase hands-per-hour.
What are SPINS?
SPINS are three-player jackpot sit-and-gos with a randomised prize pool multiplier determined before each game begins. They are fast-playing and available at several buy-in levels.
How does the cashback program work?
Cashback is earned on rake paid in cash games. The rate and tier structure are published on the PartyPoker Ontario platform. Specific amounts cannot be advertised here under AGCO rules - visit the site directly for current details.
