Editorial Policy

OntarioPoker.com covers only poker rooms that are fully registered with the AGCO and operate under iGaming Ontario. We do not review or promote unregulated or offshore poker sites.

How We Rate

Each poker room is scored across six categories: Bonus and Promotions, Software and Features, Tournament Offering, Cash Games, Payments, and Support. The overall rating is a weighted average. Ratings are updated quarterly or when operators make significant changes.

Promotion Verification

Offer details and offer terms are verified against official operator pages. We distinguish between verified public codes, offers with no public code identified, and unverified offers. We never fabricate offer details or publish offers we cannot source.

Commercial Relationships

This site contains affiliate links. If you click through and register using our links, we may receive compensation. Commercial relationships never affect editorial ratings or rankings.

AGCO-Registered Operators Only

OntarioPoker.com lists only poker rooms that hold active registration with the Alcohol and Gaming Commission of Ontario (AGCO) and operate under an agreement with iGaming Ontario. We verify registration status against the official iGO operator directory. Offshore, grey-market, and unregistered operators are excluded from all pages on this site, in full compliance with AGCO Standard 1.21.

Compliance with the CGA Code for Responsible Gaming Advertising

CGA Code Compliant

Effective January 1, 2026

The Canadian Gaming Association's Code for Responsible Gaming Advertising, administered by Ad Standards, establishes principles for integrity, transparency, and social responsibility in gambling advertising. OntarioPoker.com adheres to this Code across all content:

  • Integrity: We do not imply that gambling leads to financial improvement, social status, or success. All editorial content is factual and based on verified testing.
  • Transparency: Affiliate relationships are disclosed on every page via our above-fold disclosure bar. All promotional content is clearly identified.
  • Social responsibility: Every page on this site includes responsible gambling resources and a direct link to ConnexOntario (1-866-531-2600). We do not target minors or vulnerable populations.
  • Bonus advertising: In compliance with AGCO Standard 2.05, we do not publicly advertise specific bonus amounts, percentages, or inducements. Offer status pages direct players to operator sites for current terms.
  • Truthful representation: We do not misrepresent products, odds of winning, or present gambling as risk-free. All claims are verifiable.

Complaints about advertising on this site may be filed with Ad Standards, which administers both the CGA Code and the Canadian Code of Advertising Standards.
