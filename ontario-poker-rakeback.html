Ontario Poker Rakeback and Rewards Compared 2026

Last updated March 2026

Traditional rakeback, where a room returns a fixed percentage of rake paid, doesn't exist in Ontario's regulated market. Instead, every room runs a loyalty or rewards program that returns value through tiered systems, point conversions, and periodic promotions. The effective value you get back depends on volume, consistency, and which room you choose. GGPoker's Fish Buffet program is the strongest option for most players.

GGPoker Fish Buffet: Best Loyalty Program in Ontario

Our Pick: Best Rewards Program

GGPoker's Fish Buffet is a tiered loyalty program where you accumulate points (called PVI, Player Value Index) every time you contribute to the rake in cash games or pay tournament fees. As you reach point thresholds, you advance through levels with increasingly valuable reward chests. The program is transparent: you can always see how many points you've earned, what level you're at, and what the next reward requires.

What makes Fish Buffet stand out is its consistency. Rewards scale with your volume in a predictable way, so you know what to expect. Higher tiers unlock better chest values, and the progression doesn't require playing an unrealistic number of hours. Recreational players benefit from the lower tiers, while high-volume grinders can reach premium reward levels.

Fish Buffet rewards can be redeemed as cash or tournament tickets, giving you flexibility in how you use them. The program also resets on a regular schedule, so you need to maintain volume to stay at higher tiers.

Room-by-Room Program Details

888poker: Club 888

888poker's loyalty program uses a level-up system where you earn points through play and progress through tiers. Higher tiers unlock access to exclusive freerolls, improved reward values, and special promotions. The Made To Play challenges add extra earning opportunities on top of the base loyalty program. Club 888 is a solid option for regular players who value a mix of cash rewards and promotional extras.

PokerStars: Stars Rewards

Stars Rewards uses a chest-based system with a randomized element. You earn progress toward chests as you play, and each chest contains a reward that varies in value. The randomization means your effective return can fluctuate from chest to chest. PokerStars also offers periodic boost promotions that increase chest progress rates. The program is less predictable than GGPoker's Fish Buffet, which some players dislike, but the variance can occasionally work in your favor.

BetMGM Poker: MGM Rewards

BetMGM integrates poker into the broader MGM Rewards ecosystem. Points earned through poker can be used across sports betting, casino, and even at MGM hotel properties. This makes it uniquely valuable for players who use multiple BetMGM products. The poker-specific reward rates are competitive, though not as generous as GGPoker's program for pure poker players. If you split your time between poker and sports, the cross-platform value is hard to beat.

Read the full BetMGM review

PartyPoker: Diamond Club

PartyPoker's Diamond Club is a traditional tiered VIP program. You accumulate loyalty points and advance through levels, with each tier offering improved rewards. The Diamond Club Elite tier, designed for high-volume players, offers the best return rates. For recreational and mid-volume players, the lower tiers are less competitive than what GGPoker or 888poker offer. PartyPoker shares its network with BetMGM and Bwin, but loyalty programs are separate.

Bwin: Loyalty Points

Bwin offers a straightforward loyalty points system. You earn points as you play, and these can be exchanged for cash or tournament entries. The program is simpler than what larger rooms offer, with fewer tiers and fewer promotional extras. It's adequate for casual players but won't deliver the same value as GGPoker's or 888poker's programs for anyone playing regularly.

How Rakeback Works in Ontario's Regulated Market

In unregulated markets, some poker sites offer direct rakeback deals, returning a fixed percentage of rake. Ontario's regulated market doesn't work that way. Instead, AGCO-licensed rooms use loyalty programs that reward play indirectly. The effective return varies based on your tier, volume, and which program you're in.

Here's what this means in practice:

  • No fixed percentages: You won't see a room advertising a specific rakeback rate. Rewards scale with play, but the exact return depends on your tier level.
  • Tournaments count: Most programs include tournament fees in their loyalty calculations, not just cash game rake.
  • Consistency matters: Most programs have time-based tier resets. Playing regularly maintains your tier, while long breaks may drop you down.
  • Cash vs. tickets: Some rewards come as cash, others as tournament entries. Cash rewards offer more flexibility.

Choosing the Right Program for Your Play Style

  • High-volume cash game grinders: GGPoker's Fish Buffet offers the best scaling rewards for dedicated players. The tiered system rewards consistency and volume.
  • Tournament-focused players: GGPoker and 888poker both count tournament fees toward loyalty progress. GGPoker's larger tournament schedule gives more earning opportunities.
  • Multi-product players: If you play sports and casino alongside poker, BetMGM's cross-platform MGM Rewards is the most efficient choice.
  • Recreational players: Low-volume players benefit most from programs with strong lower tiers. GGPoker's Fish Buffet and 888poker's Club 888 are both accessible at lower play levels.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do Ontario poker sites offer rakeback?

Yes, but not as a fixed percentage. All six regulated Ontario poker sites have loyalty or rewards programs that return a portion of rake paid. GGPoker uses its Fish Buffet program, PokerStars has Stars Rewards, and BetMGM integrates with MGM Rewards. See our full rakeback and rewards guide for more detail.

Which Ontario poker room has the best rakeback program?

GGPoker's Fish Buffet program is widely regarded as the most rewarding loyalty program in Ontario. It uses a tiered system where you earn points for rake paid and unlock progressively better rewards.

How does rakeback work in the regulated Ontario poker market?

Traditional fixed-percentage rakeback isn't available. Instead, rooms offer loyalty programs that reward play with points, cashback, tournament tickets, and other benefits. The effective return depends on how much you play and which tier you reach.

Can I earn rakeback playing tournaments in Ontario?

Yes. Most Ontario poker loyalty programs count tournament fees toward your rewards. The tournament rake contribution is typically included in the buy-in and counts the same as cash game rake for loyalty program progress.
