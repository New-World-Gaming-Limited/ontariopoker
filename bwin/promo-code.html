How to Get Started

  1. Visit the official Bwin Ontario site using the link on this page. Ensure you are on the Ontario-regulated version of the platform, not an international site.
  2. Create your account. You will need to provide your legal name, date of birth, Ontario address, and a valid email. You must be 19+ and physically located in Ontario.
  3. Complete identity verification. Bwin Ontario is required by AGCO regulations to verify your identity before you can deposit or play. Have government-issued ID ready.
  4. Check for an automatic new-player offer. Some operators apply their new-player offer automatically without a code. Look for opt-in prompts during your first deposit.
  5. Make your first deposit of at least C$10 using one of the accepted methods: Interac, Visa, Mastercard. The new-player offer activates upon your qualifying deposit.
  6. Start playing. Once your deposit is confirmed, you can join cash games, tournaments, or fast-fold formats. Bonus clearing begins from your first raked hand.
